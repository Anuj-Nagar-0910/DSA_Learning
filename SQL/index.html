Key SQL Concepts Learned

Basic Querying & Filtering

Mastered SELECT statements with WHERE clauses, using operators like BETWEEN, NOT IN, and pattern matching with LIKE.

  • SELECT * with column selection
  • WHERE conditions and logical operators
  • BETWEEN and NOT IN for range and exclusion filtering
  • LIKE for pattern matching (e.g., 'A%', '%n')

Date & Time Manipulations

Worked extensively with date functions for filtering records based on time periods and calculating durations.

  • DATE_SUB, CURDATE, INTERVAL for date arithmetic
  • DATEDIFF for calculating time differences
  • YEAR() function for extracting year from dates
  • IS NULL checks for active records

Joins & Relationships

Practiced INNER JOINs to connect multiple tables and retrieve related data across entities.

  • INNER JOIN between employees, departments, salaries, titles
  • Multi-table joins for complex queries
  • DISTINCT to avoid duplicate results

Aggregations & Grouping

Used aggregate functions with GROUP BY to summarize data and analyze patterns.

  • COUNT(), AVG(), MAX(), SUM()
  • GROUP BY with multiple columns
  • ORDER BY and LIMIT for sorting and pagination

Advanced Techniques

Explored subqueries, string functions, and complex filtering for sophisticated data retrieval.

  • Subqueries in FROM and WHERE clauses
  • CONCAT() for string concatenation
  • Composite primary keys in junction tables
  • Self-referencing tables (e.g., manager hierarchy)

Practice Scenarios Covered

Employee Database Queries

Focused on HR-related queries: employee details, salaries, department assignments, and tenure calculations.

  • Employees hired 10+ years ago
  • Salary ranges and active assignments
  • Department managers with current salaries
  • Employees not in specific departments
  • Long-term department assignments

Bike Store Database Queries

Covered retail operations: customer orders, staff performance, product inventory, and sales analytics.

  • Staff members with most orders processed
  • Department employee counts
  • Hiring trends by year
  • Average salaries by job title
  • Employees with most title/salary changes
